# I can't stop thinking about Jose Zuniga’s Daily Routine Life Of A 24 Yr Old CEO 1

I followed a link from an internet website and found myself watching 'Jose Zuniga’s Daily Routine Life Of A 24 Yr Old CEO 1'.
Now I can't stop thinking about it.
'Jose Zuniga’s Daily Routine Life Of A 24 Yr Old CEO 1' is a video on youtube.
It lasts 3 minutes and 19 seconds, and purports to show the daily routine of Jose Zuniga, who is 24 years old and also a CEO.
It's a perfect artifact. Completely encapsulating a part of the online milieu in a way that makes commenting on that milieu a vain and pointless task.
This short film should be added to the archives, because in a 100 years time it will be an invaluable part of the historical record.
It is a universal madeline biscuit, a portal that can take you to a time and place, even if you've never been there.

## What happens in 'Jose Zuniga’s Daily Routine Life Of A 24 Yr Old CEO 1'
"One of my favourite ideas that I've ever heard was the thought of doing today what others aren't willing to do, so I can do tomorrow what they can't. My name's Jose Zuniga and this is a day into my life."

A cityscape, a high speed reverse tracking shot through a mall, a blue sports car speeding ominously close to a wall, some kind of bridge, and then we see him. Our first glimpse of Jose Zuniga's stomach and underwear. We note the unfamiliar brand emblazoned on said underwear. 'ESNTLS', though the E is obscured.
We also notice that Jose is topless. 

A rapid sequence of cuts, Jose descends stairs, opens a door, looks at his phone, squats, showers, then the camera luxuriously examines the blue sports car we saw earlier,  before Jose Zuniga reappears wearing a grey blazer and black t shirt. He closes the car door.
The closing of the door represents the closing of the intro of the video, a daring sally at the fourth wall that causes form and content to begin to intermingle.
